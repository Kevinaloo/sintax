OUR ORIGIN STORY

The Gospel of SinTax

I
The Idea

In 2024, a dormant NGO in Nairobi had a midnight revelation: guilt is the most underutilised currency in the world. Every day, billions of people do things they regret. What if that guilt could fund something good?

II
The Platform

We built a place where anyone can confess anonymously, get judged by an AI with the energy of a sarcastic priest, and pay a donation that matches the weight of their sin. No absolution without action.

III
The Community

The confession feed became a mirror. People discovered they weren't alone in their pettiness, their betrayals, their small daily crimes. Collective guilt became collective healing.

IV
The Mission

100% of donations fund vetted charitable causes — mental health, conflict resolution, food security. We are a registered NGO. Your sins are doing actual good.
